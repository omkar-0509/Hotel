Creamy Comfort

A traditional North Indian comfort food made with a tangy yogurt and gram flour (besan) base, simmered into a creamy curry. It’s enriched with soft, deep-fried pakoras (gram flour dumplings) and tempered with mustard seeds, cumin, garlic, and red chilies. Often served with steamed rice, it’s a flavorful and hearty vegetarian delight found in both home kitchens

Chargrilled

Tandoori Chicken is a beloved North Indian dish made by marinating chicken legs in a blend of yogurt, lemon juice, and bold spices like red chili, turmeric, and garam masala. It is then roasted in a traditional clay oven (tandoor), giving it a smoky flavor and charred edges while keeping the inside juicy and tender. Garnished with onions, lemon wedges, and fresh coriander, it’s a fiery and flavorful delicacy enjoyed across India and served in top-tier restaurants worldwide.

Richness

Dal Makhani is a rich and creamy North Indian delicacy made with whole black lentils (urad dal) and kidney beans (rajma), slow-cooked for hours to bring out deep, earthy flavors. It’s simmered with butter, cream, and aromatic spices, resulting in a smooth, velvety texture. Often served with naan or jeera rice, this indulgent dish is a staple in fine dining and loved for its comforting, luxurious taste.

Delicate

Galouti Kebab is a legendary Awadhi delicacy known for its melt-in-the-mouth texture. Traditionally made with finely minced lamb and over 100 exotic spices, the meat is marinated and cooked to such tenderness that it requires no chewing — hence the name "Galouti," meaning "soft." Originally crafted for a toothless Nawab of Lucknow, it remains a royal favorite, served with delicate parathas and mint chutney.

Spiced

Chicken Chettinad is a bold and spicy South Indian dish from the Chettinad region of Tamil Nadu. It features tender chicken pieces cooked in a fragrant masala made with freshly ground spices, roasted coconut, curry leaves, and black pepper. Known for its deep, fiery flavors and rustic aroma, it’s a favorite in upscale Indian restaurants and pairs perfectly with rice or parotta.

Aromatic

Chicken Biryani is a regal Indian dish where long-grain basmati rice is layered with marinated chicken, saffron, caramelized onions, and aromatic spices. Cooked using the dum (steam) method, the flavors meld beautifully to create a rich, fragrant, and satisfying meal. It’s often served with raita or salan and is a showstopper on any festive or fine dining menu.
